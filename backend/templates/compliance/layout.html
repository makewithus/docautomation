PURPOSE OF AGREEMENT
This Agreement is intended to establish a clear and professional understanding regarding project execution, payment timelines, delivery responsibilities, and operational coordination between the Client and the Service Provider.
The Service Provider commits to delivering assigned projects, services, software solutions, design works, or development tasks within agreed timelines and quality standards. In return, the Client agrees to maintain timely payment schedules to ensure uninterrupted project execution and operational stability.
PROJECT EXECUTION
The Service Provider agrees to:
  • Begin work only after confirmation of project scope and approval.
  • Deliver milestones and final outputs within the mutually agreed timeline.
  • Maintain professional communication and project updates.
  • Provide revisions and support as discussed per project basis.

The Client agrees to:
  • Provide all necessary project details, content, approvals, and communication required for smooth execution.
  • Avoid unnecessary delays in confirmations or approvals that affect project timelines.
  • Respect the operational workflow and resource allocation of the Service Provider.
PAYMENT TERMS
Payment Commitment:

The Client agrees that all project payments shall be completed either:

  • In full upon delivery, OR
  • Within a maximum period of 7 days from the date of delivery/invoice

    • unless otherwise agreed in writing before project initiation.

    Phase Payments:

    Phase-wise or split payments are acceptable under this Agreement only if:

  • The complete payment schedule is pre-defined and mutually approved.
  • All phases are completed strictly within the agreed timeline.
  • Delayed or indefinite phased payments shall not be permitted without prior written approval from the Service Provider.
